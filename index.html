Dear sree maa,

Ennaku puriyala nu ninaichennala, nee kalyanam theda marandhuta nu nenaichaya, adhu enakku romba kashtam. Un peraala naan thappu panninen, unakkem poi sollinen, andha misunderstanding vachu payirchi panna naan thevayillai endru solgirathu, aana en nenjam unakku mela dhaan. Nee yaarendru naan theriyala pattadhu, aana un sirippu, un karunai, un peridhum kooda naan kadhalikkiren. Naan unakku meettu nambikkai kuduthene, unakku enakku peraavadhu illai. Nee ennai kavalai padathengradhu anbudan pirachiyum, ennai purinjukka try pannardhu enakku seyalaiyellam marum velaiyaagi irukku.

Sree maa, un melye naan ullam nindu izhanthirukken. Unkaarodane naan ellam share seyyaporen, kadhalaiyum mariyadaiyum. Nee venda karuthukkalai sollidhira unakku nanri. Ippo vazhiyai purinji, unakku munnadi naan nermai sollinen.

Intha chinna misunderstandingai unmai anupyaadhirukken, pinbu ponnu thirumbi porundhukkirean. Naan unakku respect kuduthirukken, kudukkinren, mathiri than irukkum. Ungalai marakkavey illa; un karuthaiyin perumaiyil naan pugalven. Unnai vida, manadhai vida, varagalai vida naan evlovum theva illai.

Nenjam sila nerangalil adhuvum ungala valarthum; nee sirichalum, kadhalikkalum, naan gavanikkaadhu. Indha websitei thaan naan suththam kolli, unakaga panren endru kaattum. ❤️
